Our story

is a family-run business that started in 2006 at Fraser and 49th, in the heart of Vancouver’s lively Sunset neighborhood. We began by reviving a cherished local bakery, preserving its traditional recipes while adding our own modern touch. Signature treats like our “Bienenstich,” family-sized apple strudel, and handcrafted artisan rye breads are still lovingly made with the same care and tradition that started it all. Koko has grown to become more than just a neighborhood favorite—it’s a part of Vancouver’s food culture. Known for its warm atmosphere and unforgettable flavors, Koko’s reputation has spread far beyond Sunset, thanks to the enthusiasm of locals and visitors alike. From one satisfied customer to another, the buzz about Koko continues to travel, carrying our story and flavors to every corner of the city—and beyond.
